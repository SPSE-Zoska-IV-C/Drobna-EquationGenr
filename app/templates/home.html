Welcome to ExpoLoGen!



ExpoLoGen is a smart and practical web app designed to help you generate, explore, and work with exponential and logarithmic equations and functions in a fast and organized way. Whether you’re a student preparing for practice, a teacher building worksheets, or just revising key topics, ExpoLoGen gives you everything you need in one place.


With ExpoLoGen, you can easily generate new problems, view your saved history, and open detailed function views to better understand how the graphs behave. When you’re ready, you can export your selected content into a clean PDF worksheet using built-in export templates — and you can preview Template 1 and Template 2 examples right below on this page.


If you’re not sure where to start, scroll down to the User Guide at the bottom of the site for a quick step-by-step walkthrough of the main features.

Note: all the functions and equations containing logarithms are using this rule:

$$ \log_b(x) = \frac{\log(x)}{\log(b)} $$

User Guide

In the sidebar, you will find the Generate section. When you hover over it, two options appear: Equations and Functions. Click the one you want to generate, and the corresponding generation page will open.

Depending on your selection:
Equations: choose the type and difficulty level.
Functions: choose the type only.

Next, enter the number of items you want to generate. This must be a positive integer.
When you are satisfied with your selections, click the Create button to generate your equations or functions.

On the right side of the header, you will see the Export to PDF button. Click it to open the export form, which contains three dropdowns and one input field:

Type of Content: choose either Equations or Functions.
Template: select the desired PDF template.
Amount: enter the number of items you want to export - but it has to be maximum of 20 items.
Source: choose where the content comes from.

If you select Generate New, a small generation form will appear below with your chosen parameters prefilled. If you select Pick From Existing, a card will appear showing all previously generated items. Choose the items you want to include.
Finally, click Generate PDF to create your export.

The Functions page is divided into two sections:

Today’s Functions: shows only functions generated today.
History: shows all previously generated functions.

To view the graph or parameters of a function, click the Details button.

After clicking Details, you will see the Function Details page for the selected function. Here you can view:

The formula of the function.
Its type and parameters.
The graph of the function.

The inverse graph and formula of the function (displayed below the main graph).

The Equations page works similarly to Functions. It is divided into:

Today’s Equations: displays only equations generated today.
History: displays previously generated equations.

Each equation card shows the parameters used during generation. To view the solving steps and roots, click the “v” symbol on the right side of the card.
